These rules are considered your warning. Breaking the rules will result in your punishment. Ignorance is not an excuse.

If you catch someone breaking rules, do !report. If a staff member is breaking rules, make a staff report thread on the forums.

Server Rules

This server has similar rules to many other life servers with some minor differences.

It is recommended to record/stream while you play so if you encounter someone breaking the rules you have evidence for a report.


Admins have the final say, however you can try to prove your innocence!

Basic Rules

  1. Cheating, glitching, abuse and anything similar is a permantly bannable offense
  2. Targeted racism, sexism and abuse is not tolerated
  3. Toxic behavior and trolling is frowned upon and can lead to punishment

Life Rules

  1. NLR: New Life Rule, You cannot return to the place of your death within 5 minutes and you forget any interaction that happened beforehand
  2. RDM: Random Deathmatch
    • You must have an adequate reason and/or intiate to attack upon another player
    • You must have reason and/or intiate to attack / fire upon another player
  3. VDM: Vehicle Deathmatch is not tolerated however players won't recieve damage from being hit by cars

Initiation

  • You can initiate by talking, typing or ingame text
  • "Hands up or die!" is not a viable form of initiation
  • You must state your intent when initiating
  • You don't need to initiate in self-defense
  • When initiating you must give the player atleast 10 seconds to comply
  • INITIATION IS NOT REQUIRED WHEN:
    • You have already initiated within the past 5 minutes
    • They are in an illegal area (You can't engage people who aren't in the area if you are in the area)
  • YOU CANNOT INITIATE WHEN:
    • You are inside a green area (mostly city centers)
    • You can't see the person you are initiating on

Spawn Locations

There are 5 Spawn Locations on the map, where to spawn depends on your intentions and skill

  1. Georgetown
    • Very safe with no criminal activity in the center and little in the outskirts
    • Recommended for new players to get a sense of what to do and to get them rolling
    • Although no big money making spots are very close, Georgetown provides a good place to make quick money to get you off your feet
    • Has all kind of shops to get prepared for the rest of Tanoa
  2. La Rochelle
    • Minor criminal & gang activity but minimal guerilla appearance as the military airbase is nearby
    • A place for experienced players to continue making legal money
    • With garages, truck shops and an airfield nearby, La Rochelle is the perfect place to get ready for a good haul
    • Nearby are many ways to make good legal money
  3. Tanouka
    • Safe, although there are minimal police, gang and criminal encounters are very unlikely
    • A nice central location to spawn once you have a car and a plan for making money
    • Have to go South but want to avoid the Southe Port, spawn here
  4. Blue Pearl Industrial
    • The hub of industry on Tanoa not only importing and exporting goods, but drugs and guns as well
    • Good for players who want to move illegal goods
    • Looking to make a bit more money with no regard to your own safety, illegal good transporting almost always starts here
  5. Southern Port
    • A Semi-lawless city where criminals and gang members are commonplace however police can be active on occasion
    • Unrecommended unless you have a way to protect yourself or a ride to nearby locations
    • High Risk, Low Reward
